Shazam is an online hackathon organized by the tech leading company Codestacks. The aim of this competition is to inculcate good development etiquette among young developers of India. We believe setting your hands on projects help you achieve better programming skills and also help you with your career. You are free to use any technology and language for development of projects


Codestacks

  • Candidates could participate in teams or as an individual. There should be minimum 2 candidates in a team and maximum 5.

  • Cash prize would be awarded to top 3 teams. Prize amount would be disclosed shortly.
